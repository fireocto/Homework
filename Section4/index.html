Brewing Your Next Favorite Beer

Why Brew Your Own Beer?

Brewing your own beer has many advantages! First of all, beer at the store continues to get more expensive. Secondly, and most importantly: the best tasting beer is the one you made yourself! Brewing isn't that hard, and I'll walk you through each step to making your next favorite beer!

What You'll Need

  • Large pot, at least 3 gallons -- This is the pot I use
  • Grains
  • Hops
  • Yeast -- Here's my favorite
  • Water

Once you have all your gear and ingredients, just follow the list of steps below to make your beer!

How You Do It

  1. Heat the water to 180F. Take off the heat and mix in your DME, making sure it is completely dissolved. You have now created wort!
  2. Place pot back on the heat and bring to a boil.
  3. Once the wort is up to a boil, follow this hopping schedule:
    • 0.5oz CTZ - 60
    • 1oz Amarillo - 30
    • 1oz Citra - 5
    • 1oz each Citra and Amarillo - 0
  4. Once the 60 minute boil is over, cool the wort to 67F.
  5. Now that the wort is cool, pitch your yeast of choice and make sure it gets completely rehydrated.
  6. Store is a cool, dark place for 7-10 days.
  7. Once fermentation is complete, package your beer, carbonate it to 2.5 volumes, and enjoy!
